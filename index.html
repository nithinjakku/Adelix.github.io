What do we do?

At Adelix we surrender ourselves to develop a highly robust and efficient UAV ecosystem. We are not constrained to a particular sector of UAV, we are everywhere from R&D to operations. We see ourselves as a DAAS (drone a service) & SAAS (Software as a service) company. we continuously strive to bring breathtaking inventions to life to perform countless operations while creating opportunities to many individuals. We build AI to create jobs and not to take them away. At Adelix we do R&D on many essential technologies, we manufacture and assemble our systems in INDIA. We develop multiple software applications and interfaces UTM & other service portals to maintain our airspace diverse and safe. We fully operate from INDIA with our foot extended in countries like USA & Japan. We aim to be a crucial part of the growth story of the INDIAN drone ecosystem.

MEDICINE

The delay in emergency medical supply leads to thousands of deaths every single day. Hundreds of families are getting pushed into catastrophic situations due to these death. Whatever the reason of death, the financial stability of the family will be drastically disturbed. To help this situation governments and private agencies are trying their best but yet the situations is nothing better. Adelix is using powerful drone based ecosystem not only to make the process faster, but also to make it affordable and efficient. These medical deliveries can be categorized as, emergency supplies to hospitals, emergency supplies to public, prescription delivery etc... Medical deliveries will be given priority in the first place in our entire ecosystem in the country, enabling quick reach to the various locations. Life matters...Learn more

FOOD

Food orders have been on a boom since the commence of online food delivery. But the pain point in every delivery is the cost that the food delivery company implies on us to deliver the food to our door step. It is always hard to see high costs in the cost breakdown in the order bill. We often try for free food delivery but it is not possible to make it all the times unless you subscribe to some of the plans that the company offer you. Here comes Adelix to serve your food hot/cold in fraction of minutes. We tie-up with companies like zomato and swiggy to get your food to your door step faster and cheaper than the process it was before. We take advantage of our highly capable drone ecosystem which enables us to do swift and affordable deliveries. Time & Wealth matters...Learn more

E-COMMERCE

Since 2000 E-commerce has become the most reliable sector on which people all around the world depends to get their required stuff home. While the other side of the E-commerce is a hectic process which directly reflects in the cost of the product. The costliest part in the process of bringing the product to your door step is the last mile delivery. This last mile delivery cost can be drastically reduced by using an efficient drone delivery supply ecosystem by Adelix. Also huge carbon emissions are emitted during the process of delivery which harms the environment, by using the delivery ecosystem which is fully electric, the harm to the climate eventually reduces. The drones will be fully electric, supporting the goal of net zero emissions. Climate matters...Learn more

The quick commerce has been under a boom using the opportunity of bring these groceries to public in span of minutes but by putting additional charges in the bill. The vehicle that is used to deliver will incur huge carbon emissions in a life time that takes away our good health. Here comes Adelix with a smart drone based ecosystem solution for all the cost and environment related issues.
